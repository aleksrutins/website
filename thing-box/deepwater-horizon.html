Deepwater Horizon

April 20-22, 2010
Amount of Oil Spilled
136M - 206M gallons
Location
Deepwater Horizon Oil Rig, Mississippi Canyon, Gulf of Mexico

Impact

The Deepwater Horizon spill disintegrated an island and killed over 800,000 birds and close to 65,000 turtles. Some endangered species were completely killed off.

Cleanup

The Deepwater Horizon oil spill was cleaned up by dispersion, and then burning or skimming the remaining oil.
